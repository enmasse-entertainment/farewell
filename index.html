Thank you for eleven great years!

En Masse Entertainment began more than 11 years ago. We started out as a small team, committed to publishing the highest quality games. Along the way we built a world-class community, who showed nothing but love for the content and worlds we created. Because of that support we were able to continue to build games that we are proud of with the best people in the industry.

On August 17 we made the difficult announcement that we would begin the process of discontinuing the business of En Masse Entertainment. Our focus in the coming weeks and months will be on our team, helping them to find new opportunities, and on our games, to ensure a seamless transition for you.

The En Masse games you have come to love will continue. We are working with our developers to transfer your in-game data to a new home when the time comes. We are fully committed to making this the best possible experience we can.

EMP currency purchases have been disabled. If you currently have an EMP balance you can continue to spend your EMP in TERA and Closers until those products transfer over to their new publishers.

New game account creation has been disabled for Closers and TERA on PC. All current game accounts can continue to log in to their account and play TERA and Closers until those products transfer over to their new publishers.
